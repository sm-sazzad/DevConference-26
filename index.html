Who can attend DevConference 2026?

Developers, designers, tech leads, students, and anyone passionate about technology. Tickets are available for both in-person and virtual attendance.

Early bird tickets start at $149 until July 31. Regular admission is $249. Group discounts (5+) are available — contact us for details.

Absolutely! We have 12+ workshops covering AI, cloud, security, and full-stack. Separate registration required for some workshops.

Yes, we follow a strict code of conduct to ensure a safe and inclusive environment for everyone. It's available on our official site.
